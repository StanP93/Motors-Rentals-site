Guided Desert Quad Safari Tour (350CC)

+2

Service Description

We are providing a complete package through multiple activities on the 4-hour Quad Desert Safari: Sandboarding, Camels & Pictures. Drive across the sand dunes by ATV (Quad), you can enjoy a camel ride, sandboarding as well as many other exciting activities.

Starting with the pick up from your hotel in a 4x4 Vehicle you will head off with your local guide to the desert. After a 45min-60min drive from Dubai, you will reach the Lahbab Red Dunes desert, you will get 20–25 minutes to relax and to do your camel ride there.

Restroom and toilet facilities are also available.

Once you are ready, the ride into the red dunes with your own Quadbike (Kawasaki- 350CC) will start. Instruction and safety-gear will be provided. Continuing with 1 hour Quadbiking is an unforgettable experience. Your guide will help you to find the best track in desert. You can also try sandboarding, a popular desert activity and take as many pictures as you want in the beautiful unlimited desert.

After your Activity we will provide you with cold water & softdrinks and bring you back to your Hotel safely.

Good to know before the Tour:

• The duration of this activity is 4-hours which includes pick up and drop off

• Quad Bikes segment is around 1 h

• Quad biking is solely the participant’s responsibility. Guests are

recommended to take precautions when driving as any damage, injuries sustained, or

items lost during quad biking are not covered by insurance.

• We shall not be responsible for any kind of loss, accident, sickness or injury.

• All participants must sign an Indemnity Form before commencing the activity

Included:

• pick up* and drop off*

• Quads and safety gear

• water & softdrinks

• short camel ride (can be repeated)

• sandboarding

• photographs in the desert

Not suitable for:

• Children under 13 years

• Pregnant women

• People with back problems

• People with heart problems

Pick Up Service (Transportation):

• Pickup is available from all Hotels in Dubai & Port Rashid

• Pickup with extra charge is availible from Al Maha Resort, Jebel Ali, Bab Al Shams, Discovery garden and Investment City

​• Depending on your Location the pick-up time will be in the first 30 minutes of your selected Time

​• If there is any change we will notifie you via WhatsApp, phone call or email
